Our Mission

Our mission is to obtain a fair share in the market for fleet management and monitoring. While aiming for profitability, we also ensure that customer satisfaction comes first on our agenda.
We believe that a satisfied customer will is a happy client. Thus the higher the chances of getting more.

Our Vision

To be the number ONE Security Company in Kenya and all over Africa by offering new and innovative service options.We also aim to provide tools for monitoring the global positioning system and fleet management that increases revenues, productivity and reduces losses to your Company.
Our vision is based on a workforce that is sufficiently motivated and supported by years of experience in the area of GPS vehicle tracking.
